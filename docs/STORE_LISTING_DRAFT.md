# Stress Bubble Store Listing Draft

## Name

Stress Bubble

## Short Description

Pop bubbles in your New Tab or toolbar popup for a quick stress-relief break.

## Full Description

Stress Bubble turns your Chrome New Tab into a fast, lightweight bubble-popping mini-game built for short mental breaks.

Use the New Tab experience for full play sessions, or launch the popup for a quick round without leaving your current browsing flow.

Current MVP features:

- quick-play bubble popping gameplay
- three modes: `classic`, `rage`, `zen`
- combo-based scoring and stress meter
- local progression with best scores, coins, streaks, and achievements
- cosmetic unlocks and basic customization
- accessibility options such as reduced motion and high contrast

Stress Bubble stores gameplay progress locally in your browser and does not require host permissions.
